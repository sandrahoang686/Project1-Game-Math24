Math 24 is an arithmetical card game and experienced it's peak in the 1990s. The point of the same is to make the number, 24, with all four numbers but there are many versions of the game. The original version allows +, -, *, ÷, and () operations!

~wikipedia

You will be playing the original version! Make the number, 24, and the only operations you can use are +, -, *, ÷, and (). You must use all four numbers and only once. Remember the operations of math!
